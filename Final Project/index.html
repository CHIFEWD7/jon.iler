    • Simply, Music.

    Concise is a group of people that love good music, and a home for artists to express themselves. We pride ourselves on being more than just a label and taking the time to find and nurture new talent. We run a studio locally in Chicago and offer music production lessons to people who are passionate about creating music, and offer them a platform to share their music with the world. As a service to our artists, our entire talent roster is available to book through our site.

    Think of Concise less as a label, and more as an artist collective. Our primary interest is bringing the music we hear in our heads to the world. We'd like to share our creations with you. As we progress through our infancy in this big world of music, we hope to showcase everything from the dance floor friendly to the unrecognizeable.
